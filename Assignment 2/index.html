Happiness Level Around the World

Happiness Level of the population are important in any countries in the world as they can be a very good parameter to estimate the well-being of the population in a country. The Happiness Score used in this visualisation is estimated by using several values of a country as estimators and factors for the well-being of the population. The estimators are as follows: GDP per capita, Healthy Life Expectancy, Social support, Freedom to make life choices, Generosity and Corruption Perception.

Countries with highest happiness scores

With that, the best countries with the highest happiness scores are shown in the bar chart on the right. Those with a higher rankings in happiness score are more possible to be a more advance country, hence are more possible to provide a better quality of life to its population. For example, the countries such as Sweden, Norway and Finland, which are shown on the bar chart, are located close to each other on the world map, while having high happiness scores. This can be lead by similar values shared on the region or the population.

Countries far away from each others

These are the countries that are located at different places on the world map. The happiness varies accordingly, especially higher on most of the Western Regions. The results can be seen on the bar chart at the side.
